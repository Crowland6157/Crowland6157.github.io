BIO

Jarod Anthony Higgins was born on December 2nd, 1998 in Chicago, Illinois. Growing up in the Calmut Park area, Jarad explored the world of Rap and in time, emerged as an up and coming singer/Songwriter. Eventually, he came out with the name Juice WRLD and the name became progressivly known world wide! Juice WRLD came out with multiple billboard breaking songs during his career and some songs have been streamed over one billion times on muliple platforms. His time in the industry was short, but the impact he left will continue to change our music world for years to come!
